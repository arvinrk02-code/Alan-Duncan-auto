What we do

Whatever the warning light — or the noise, or the MOT letter — we'll sort it. Hover a light to see it clear.

  • Servicing

    Interim and full services to keep your car running right and holding its value.

  • MOT prep & repairs

    MOT preparation and any repairs to get you through — told straight, no upsell.

  • Diagnostics

    Warning light on? We plug in, find the fault, and explain it in plain English.

  • Brakes, clutches & tyres

    Brakes, clutches, tyres and tracking — the bits that keep you safe on Moray’s roads.

  • Batteries & electrics

    Flat battery, starting trouble or dodgy wiring — traced and sorted.

  • Engine & DPF cleaning

    Engine carbon cleaning, DPF and injector cleaning to bring back power and economy.

Also aircon, breakdown recovery and bumper & accident repairs. Not sure what’s wrong? Give us a call — we’ll have a look and tell you honestly.
