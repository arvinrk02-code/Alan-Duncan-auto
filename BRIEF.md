## Design direction (locked)
Clean & trustworthy, executed through an **engineering-blueprint / neo-brutalist**
aesthetic (client-supplied reference: the Timescale design system on refero).
- Carbon black + warm paper (#fafaf7) + one signal-orange accent (#ff5b29).
- Geist (interface) + Geist Mono (every numeral: phone, prices, mileage, hours).
- Hard 5px offset shadows, graph-paper grid, black-line technical car drawing.
- Recurring motif: stylized UK number-plate chip (header CTA + car regs).
Full system lives in `css/styles.css` `:root`. See README for build/deploy.
